# **Being Fruitful**

## Key Scriptures

> "So, my brothers and sisters, you also died to the law through the body of Christ, that you might belong to another, to Him who was raised from the dead, in order that we might **bear fruit** for God."
> — Romans 7:4

> "For the earnest expectation of the creature waiteth for the **manifestation of the sons of God**."
> — Romans 8:19

> "He that spared not his own Son, but delivered him up for us all, how shall he not with him also freely **give us all things**?"
> — Romans 8:32

---

# How to Be Fruitful

## 1. Focus on Jesus

> "For the woman who has a husband is bound by the law to her husband as long as he lives."
> — Romans 7:2 (NKJV)

> "So then if, while her husband lives, she marries another man, she will be called an **adulteress**."
> — Romans 7:3 (NKJV)

> "Therefore, my brethren, you also have become **dead to the law** through the body of Christ, that you may be **married to another**—to Him who was raised from the dead, that we should bear fruit to God."
> — Romans 7:4 (NKJV)

> "For when we were in the **flesh**, the sinful passions which were aroused by the law were at work in our members to bear fruit to **death**."
> — Romans 7:5 (NKJV)

> "But now we have been **delivered from the law**, having died to what we were held by, so that we should serve in the newness of the Spirit and not in the oldness of the letter."
> — Romans 7:6 (NKJV)

---

## 2. Do Not Be Lukewarm

> "So then because thou art **lukewarm**, and neither cold nor hot, I will spue thee out of my mouth."
> — Revelation 3:16

> "No man putteth a piece of **new cloth** unto an **old garment**."
> — Matthew 9:16

> "Neither do men put **new wine** into **old bottles**."
> — Matthew 9:17

---

## 3. Eat Solid Food

> "For ye have not received the spirit of bondage again to fear; but ye have received the Spirit of **sonship**."
> — Romans 8:15

> "For the earnest expectation of the creature waiteth for the manifestation of the **sons of God**."
> — Romans 8:19

> "For every one that useth **milk** is unskilful in the **word of righteousness**."
> — Hebrews 5:13

> "But **strong meat** belongeth to them that are of **full age**."
> — Hebrews 5:14

> "Wherefore thou art **no more a servant**, but **a son**."
> — Galatians 4:7

> "But after that faith is come, we are **no longer under a schoolmaster**."
> — Galatians 3:25

---

## 4. It Is Free

> "He that spared not his own Son, but delivered him up for us all, how shall he not with him also **freely** give us all things?"
> — Romans 8:32

> "For if by one man's offence death reigned by one; much more they which receive abundance of grace and of the **gift** of righteousness shall **reign in life** by one, Jesus Christ."
> — Romans 5:17

---

## Walking in the Flesh vs. Walking in the Spirit

> "For when **we were in the flesh**, the sinful passions which were aroused by the law were at work in our members to bear fruit to death."
> — Romans 7:5

> "This only I want to learn from you: Did you receive the Spirit by the works of the **law**, or by the hearing of **faith**? Are you so foolish? Having **begun in the Spirit**, are you now being made perfect by the **flesh**?"
> — Galatians 3:2–3

> "Therefore He who supplies the Spirit to you and works miracles among you, does He do it by the works of the **law**, or by the hearing of **faith**?—just as Abraham 'believed God, and it was accounted to him for righteousness.'"
> — Galatians 3:5–6
